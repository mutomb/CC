What it does?

The app allows you to select color(s), add as many as you wish to a cart and finally check out those color(s)

How to choose color(s)?

First, press on a circle corresponding to the color that you want.
Second, press on the "Add to Cart".
Third, Press "-" or "+" to increase or decrease repectively the amount of the chosen color.
Fourth, Press "Agree" to confirm the choice or otherwise "cancel" when you don't want to add the color.

Can You add different colors to cart?

Yes. Do not press "Checkout Now". Instead, press on a different color then add it to the same cart.

How do you checkout color(s) ?

After Choosing color(s) and their corresponding quantity, then press "Checkout".
